The Prayer of Lady Pickles


Our Froggy Pickles, who art in the pond,
Hoppin' be thy game.
Thy lily pad come, thy froggy fun be done,
On land as it is in water.

Give us this day our daily flies,
And forgive us for the occasional leap.
As we forgive those who ribbit against us,
And lead us not into the beaks of herons,
But deliver us from tadpole troubles.

For thine is the swamp, the croak, and the glory,
Forever and ever, ribbit!
Ribbit! Ribbit!
